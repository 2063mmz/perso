Conception d’un générateur de packs d’assistance pour un assistant mobile hors ligne destiné aux techniciens de maintenance.

RAG

Project framework

1Collect — identify useful maintenance documents from work orders and CMMS data.
2Extract — parse PDF/DOCX/JSON sources, tables and images, with OCR fallback.
3Structure — clean, chunk and enrich content with metadata and provenance.
4Index — generate embeddings, searchable indexes and retrieval metadata.
5Package — export compact assistance packs for offline mobile integration.
